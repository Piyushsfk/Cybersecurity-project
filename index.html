Key Findings

Scale of Threats:

  • Total Detections: Over 400 million detections across approximately 8.5 million Endpoints.
  • Detection Rate: Averaging 761 detections per minute.
  • Behaviour-based detections: ~49 million detections constituting 12.5% of the total detections.

Ransomware and Malware Incidents:

  • Ransomware incident ratio: ~1 per 650 detections
  • Malware incident ratio: ~1 per 38,000 detections
  • Cryptojacking: Emerging as a significant threat with over 5.28 million detections in a year.

Malware Attack Spectrum:

  • Dominant Threats: Trojans (41%) and Infectors (33%) contribute significantly to the attack spectrum.
  • Geographical Hotspots (States): Telangana (15%) and Tamil Nadu (14%) top the list in terms of malware detections.
  • City-wise Analysis: Surat (15%) and Bengaluru (14%) report the highest number of detections.

Attack Vectors:

  • Distribution of Detections: Over 50% of detections are associated with removable media and network drives.
  • Detection Rate: Averaging 761 detections per minute.
  • Malicious Links:Approximately 25% of attacks result from clicking on malicious links in emails and websites.

Top Three Industries:

  • The Automobile Industry experiences the highest number of detections, followed by the Government and Education sector.

Mobile Threat Landscape:

  • Android Devices: An average of ~3 attacks per month per Android device were observed for the year 2023.
